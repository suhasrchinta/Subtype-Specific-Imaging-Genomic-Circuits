How to read this

What this page shows — and doesn't

Real data, real patients

Every scan, gene-correlation point, and AUC value on this page comes directly from the I-SPY2/ACRIN-6698 cohort (n=384) and its linked GSE194040 gene expression data (n=988). Nothing here is simulated.

Mediation, not causation

The paper's circuits are established via formal bootstrapped mediation analysis, not simple correlation. Some relationships shown here (like SCUBE2's) are directionally consistent but statistically weaker than the strongest circuit (ERBB2) — that nuance is preserved rather than smoothed over.

TNBC's circuit is different in kind

FLT1 and ANGPT2 are vascular/microenvironment genes, not tumor-intrinsic like SCUBE2 or ERBB2. Background parenchymal enhancement (BPE) shows no significant genome-wide gene correlation on its own — the TNBC circuit is established at the pathway level (GSEA), which this page's scatter plot honestly reflects as a weak direct relationship.

Preliminary findings

HR+/HER2− results are based on only 24 pCR-positive cases and are reported as preliminary throughout the source study. This page carries that same caveat forward.
